About Me

Hi and welcome! I’m Mina and I help businesses grow their social media through developing content strategies that work for them. I used to be a journalist, then I became a social media specialist and digital creator in 2016. I have worked as a social media specialist with marketing agencies and different brands since then. On the side, I have always enjoyed blogging in my field of expertise. Academic studies on social media have also been interesting for me! I studied women’s self-expression on Instagram for my MA thesis and published a book about it. Currently based in Vancouver, I study communication, follow my social media passion through blogging and continue helping small businesses to grow. Publications

Instagram; A window to the women’s world

I wrote my book “Instagram; A window to the women’s world” based on a research that I did for my MA thesis. I analyzed how Iranian women use social media especially Instagram to express themselves. Women are now in charge of pages with thousands of followers, unlike the past when others including film directors, writers, etc. talked instead of them and represented their lives in media. They now have the opportunity of self-representation and they are using the pictures and words to share their own narrative of their world. They use objects in their photos to show what groups they belong to and what interests they have. In my book, I have interviewed 32 active female users of Instagram and analyzed their content to find out how they have used the platform to express their lives and shape a picture of themselves.
